# Bajaj Auto Stock Market Analysis using Tableau

### Transforming Stock Market Data into Actionable Business Insights

This project presents a comprehensive Tableau dashboard that analyzes **Bajaj Auto Ltd.'s** historical stock market performance through interactive visualizations. It explores stock price trends, trading volume, volatility, investor participation, price-volume relationships, and manufacturing footprint using a storytelling approach.

</div>

---



# Project Overview

This project leverages Tableau to transform historical stock market data into meaningful business insights through interactive dashboards. The analysis focuses on stock price trends, market volatility, trading activity, investor participation, and manufacturing operations using a structured storytelling approach.

---

# Business Objectives

- Analyze long-term stock price trends.
- Study daily market behaviour.
- Measure rolling market volatility.
- Evaluate annual trading activity.
- Analyze monthly trading patterns.
- Compare deliverable volume with total trading volume.
- Detect price-volume outliers.
- Understand investor participation across months.
- Visualize Bajaj Auto's manufacturing footprint.
